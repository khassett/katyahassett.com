Spell It

UI/UX | October 2021

Many children find learning to spell boring and repetitive, and struggle to retain words through reading and writing alone.

We created "Spell It," a speech recognition mobile app where kids can learn to spell in a playful, engaging way.

User Testing (Round 2)

To validate the design we teste the app with 7 users via Maze.

We asked users to complete three tasks:

  • Sign up and complete a lesson
  • Play a game
  • View your stats
  • Add a child to their account

Final Thoughts and Future Iterations

Testing participants provided essential insights and suggestions. We would like to continue to provide voice assistance to teach kids to spell in an engaging way.

New Features:

  • Connect with friends
  • Add more lessons and games
  • Parent profiles
  • Invite friends to play games with you
  • Add customized spelling list
  • Add time intervals to monitor time spent
