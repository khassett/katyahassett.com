Timeframe: 3 weeks

Many children find learning to spell boring and repetitive, and struggle to retain words through reading and writing alone.

We created "Spell It," a speech recognition mobile app where kids can learn to spell in a playful, engaging way.

High fidelity mobile prototype: Prototype

Empathize
The Power of Voice

Studies show that oral language development is an effective way of learning how to spell. This type of learning is particularly beneficial for students with dyslexia and dysgraphia.
