Hi, I'm Katya

I'm a graphic designer with a focus on branding, UI/UX, and digital systems that feel human. I’m currently finishing my BFA in Graphic Design at Portland State University and hold a UI/UX certificate from UC Berkeley.

I’ve worked on a wide range of projects — from building websites for educators to designing campaign materials and developing brand systems for small businesses. My focus is always on making things that are not only visually strong but also clear, usable, and rooted in strategy.

I'm comfortable in both design and front-end development spaces, using tools like Figma, Adobe CC, HTML/CSS, and WordPress to bring ideas to life. I’ve also led projects from concept through launch and enjoy collaborating with editors, developers, and other creatives to make meaningful things.

I'm open to full-time roles, freelance projects, and collaborations — especially with teams driven by purpose, inclusivity, and good design. Let’s connect!
