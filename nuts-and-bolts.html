Nuts and Bolts Subscription Service

Brand Design | March 2025

This branding project for RetroSupply Co. centered on creating a cohesive visual identity for a new subscription service rooted in vintage aesthetics and modern digital tools. I worked on a team to develop a full design system that included:

  • Primary and secondary logos
  • Lettermarks and responsive wordmarks
  • Contained logo systems (vertical and horizontal)
  • Application of brand assets on busy backgrounds
  • Monthly product image template
  • Custom typography and refined color palette with pairings
  • Primary and secondary illustrations, including a responsive pictorial mark
  • Icon system and repeatable patterns
  • Comprehensive mockups showcasing all brand elements in context
