Output Poster Series

Coding Project | March 2025

This project explores the intersection of code, nature, and design. Using Processing, I created a series of generative visuals inspired by organic movement and technical patterning. Each animation was developed through custom algorithms and translated into a still poster composition. The final works were showcased through large-scale projections and a printed poster series.

Tools
