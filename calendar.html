Illustrative Calendar

This illustrated calendar features a series of original alien characters, each inspired by a different planet in our solar system. Drawn entirely in Procreate, the project explores imaginative life forms that embody the unique traits, colors, and atmospheres of each celestial body.

The final layout is a 6-month calendar that brings together these interplanetary illustrations into a vibrant calendar.
