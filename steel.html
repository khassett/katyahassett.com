The Task
Self-Guided Kiosk

I was assigned to design and build a self-guided kiosk. I decided to design a kiosk for a piercing shop.

Define
User Personas
Goals

You’re testing this kiosk as Juno, a 26 year old who wants to get their nose pierced as soon as possible.

You want a septum piercing with a seamless hoop.

User Flow
Prototype user test script
Scenario

Imagine you are a person going to get their nose pierced. It is 1:00 pm on a Saturday.

Clear Goals

1. You need to decide if you want a hoop or a stud in your nose
2. You want to get pierced as soon as possible
3. You want to know it’s metal you aren’t allergic to

Situational Context

It’s crowded in the shop and you see a lot of options.

Prompts and Questions

What did you notice when you walked in?
Do you feel comfortable or hesitant to use a kiosk to book your piercing? Why?

Challenges

You’re feeling overwhelmed by the options. You have never been to this shop before and aren't familiar with the piercing artists.
