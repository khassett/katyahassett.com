AOMTrading is a site for people to learn how to trade through class subscriptions. I worked with the founder, Alex Minkovich, to design and implement features for a clean user experience.

I designed class pages, worked on user flows, and integrated my designs into WordPress.

Empathize

In order to understand the wants and needs of the company, Alex and I went over the users of the companies, so I could understand how users would interact with the site. The goal of this website is to provide access to educational trading recources. The challenge is taking complicated concepts and making it more easily approachable for users to begin their journey to learn how to trade. It's important to build trust, confidence, and ease of use for the clients.
