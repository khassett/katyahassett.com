AOM Trading

Category

WordPress, UI/UX, Web Design

Posted

December 2020

AOM Trading is a site for people to learn how to trade through class subscriptions. I worked with the founder, Alex Minkovich, to design and implement what he wanted featured on the website.

I designed class pages, worked on user flows, and integrate my designs into WordPress. This is one of my first project, and it showcases my expertise in WordPress. I have learned a lot since this project.

Skills:WordPress HTML CSS Adobe Premiere

Empathize

I wanted to understand the wants and needs of the company so I could design a flow for users. The goal of this website is to provide access to educational trading recources. We are taking something complicated and making it more easily digestable for people to learn and retain.

Competitive Analysis

Alex and I walked through competitor sites to get a sense of how we could organize the information.

Learning WordPress

Learning WordPress plugins and integrations took a while for me to grasp.

We are a digital agency with 20 plus years of collective experience in business like this takes much more effort.

User Insights

“Kids get irritated if their parents force them to practice spelling.”

Video Editing

I used Adobe Premiere and iMovie to edit class videos for AOMTrading. I would then post these videos onto AOMtrading.com

Results

After collecting user insights, we learned that customers want supplemental one-on-one help so we worked on integrating tutoring into the AOM Trading website. It is not a class substitution but rather a clarification of classroom materials.
